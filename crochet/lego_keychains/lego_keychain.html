Lego Keychains

I decided to do this project, because I wanted to give a key-chain to all the members of my research group.
It was a very fun project for me, where I learned to crochet squares,
that can handle getting wet
Time: It took me 5 to 6 hours per key-chain.

here will I place tags and maybe also an about section

Materials

materials overview

yarn

yarn specifications

hobby vlies

Yarn was found at a garage sale.
Crochet hook was, I believe, 1.1 mm, found at a thrift store
Hobby vlies was bought at Selfmade (link)
Key chains where bought at Selfmade (link)
I highly recommend their key chains, they are very strong and sturdy
I believe the rings between the key chain and crochet lego brick are o-rings bought at Selfmade (link)
They come in multiple colors like the key chains.
I do not remember where I got the pliers from. I know you can buy them at Panduro.
I used the pliers for adding the o-rings and also to pull the needle through when my fingers got tired and sore.
